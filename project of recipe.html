3.Roti Recipe

To make delicious roti, a staple in Indian cuisine, start by mixing 2 cups of whole wheat flour with a pinch of salt in a bowl. Gradually add water and knead the mixture into a soft, pliable dough. Let it rest for about 15-20 minutes. Then, divide the dough into small, equal-sized portions and roll them into balls. Take one ball, flatten it slightly, and dust it with some flour. Roll it out into a thin, round disc using a rolling pin. Heat a griddle or tava over medium-high heat and place the rolled-out roti on it. Cook for about 1-2 minutes on one side until you see bubbles forming, then flip it over and cook the other side until it puffs up and has golden brown spots. Serve hot with your favorite curry or vegetables. Enjoy your homemade roti!
